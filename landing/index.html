loopclub is the online platform for writing music with other musicians in real time. It’s an on-chain drum machine — start a session in your browser, build loops together cell by cell with anyone, anywhere, and every beat you add is yours.

How does loopclub work?

  1. 1 Start a session Open loopclub in your browser — nothing to download or install — and spin up a fresh step-sequencer grid.
  2. 2 Invite your collaborators Share a link. Other musicians join remotely from anywhere and play in the same session with you in real time.
  3. 3 Write and produce together Layer loops, light up cells, and build a full beat as a group. Every cell you contribute is provably yours on-chain on MegaETH.
